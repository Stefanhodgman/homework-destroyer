# Homework Destroyer
## A Roblox Clicker/Simulator Game

![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-in%20development-yellow)
![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-Roblox-red)
![Genre](https://img.shields.io/badge/genre-Clicker%20%7C%20Simulator-blue)

**Ever wanted to destroy your homework?** Now you can! Homework Destroyer is a satisfying clicker/simulator game where players destroy homework to earn coins, unlock powerful weapons, collect pets, and progress through multiple school zones.

Click to destroy homework papers, earn Destruction Points (DP), and become the ultimate Homework Destroyer!

- **Click-to-Destroy Mechanic** - Satisfying destruction with visual effects
- **50+ Unique Weapons** - From pencils to nuclear erasers
- **15+ Collectible Pets** - Auto-destroy homework and provide bonuses
- **10 Unique Zones** - Classroom to The Void dimension
- **Rebirth & Prestige Systems** - Deep progression mechanics
- **Daily Rewards & Events** - Keep players coming back
- **Mobile Friendly** - Optimized for all devices

## ğŸ¨ Game Features

### Zones
1. **The Classroom** (Starter) - Classic school setting
2. **The Library** - Towering bookshelves, quiet chaos
3. **The Cafeteria** - Lunch rush destruction
4. **Computer Lab** - Digital homework destruction
5. **Gymnasium** - Sports-themed carnage
6. **Music Room** - Rhythmic destruction
7. **Art Room** - Creative chaos
8. **Science Lab** - Explosive experiments
9. **Principal's Office** - Boss battle zone
10. **The Void** (Secret) - Endgame dimension

### Weapon Tiers
- **Common** - Basic school supplies (Pencil, Eraser)
- **Uncommon** - Enhanced tools (Red Pen, Scissors)
- **Rare** - Powered equipment (Electric Sharpener)
- **Epic** - Advanced tech (Laser Pointer, Shredder)
- **Legendary** - Reality-bending items (Tesla Coil Pen)
- **Mythic** - Ultimate destruction (Nuclear Eraser)
- **SECRET** - Hidden endgame weapons
